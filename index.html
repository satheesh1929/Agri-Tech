IoT Failure Learning Zone

Common failures in Smart Irrigation systems and how to fix them.

Sensor Drift

Moisture sensor showing 0% even in wet soil.

  • RCA: Soil salinity corrosion on probes.
  • Fix: Clean probes with sandpaper or replace with capacitive sensor.

Gateway Disconnect

No data upload to cloud for > 1 hour.

  • RCA: Power fluctuation or SIM card expiry.
  • Fix: Install UPS for gateway & check data plan.

Valve Stuck Open

Water flowing despite "OFF" command.

  • RCA: Solenoid valve diaphragm debris blockage.
  • Fix: Open valve head and clean the diaphragm spring.

NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index)

NDVI helps measure crop health from the sky. Green plants reflect infrared light!

  • High NDVI: Healthy, vibrant crop.
  • Low NDVI: Stressed or dead crop.

Myth vs Science

Debunking common agricultural misconceptions.

Myth

"More Fertilizer = More Yield"

Science

Excess fertilizer causes soil salinity and burns roots. Follow NPK ratio!

Myth

"Chemicals are always bad"

Science

Judicious use of chemicals saves crops from total loss during severe outbreaks.
